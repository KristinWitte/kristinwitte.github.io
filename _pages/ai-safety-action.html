Abgeordnetenwatch

Abgeordnetenwatch.de lets you post a public question to any member of the German Bundestag or the European Parliament. Your question and the representative's response are visible to everyone, creating public accountability.

You paste two things: a short question (max 300 characters) into the question field, and a longer message (max 1,000 characters) as supporting context.

Works for members of the Bundestag and the European Parliament. Find your representative at abgeordnetenwatch.de.

PREPARE YOUR TALKING POINTS

Answer these before you call. You don't need to read from them; they help you speak with clarity and confidence.

1. What specific AI risk do you want to raise?
e.g. loss of control, job displacement, AI-driven disinformation, cyberattack risks
2. Why does this matter to you personally?
e.g. your profession, your family, your community
3. What specific action do you want them to commit to?
e.g. support a national AI risk strategy, push for adequate oversight resources
4. Do you have a direct question for them?
e.g. 'Do you support a national AI risk strategy?'

TIPS FOR YOUR CALL

  • State your name and that you're a constituent right at the start.
  • Keep it under 2 minutes; be focused and specific.
  • If unavailable, leave a message with your name and one clear ask.
  • Be respectful; you're more likely to be taken seriously.
  • Follow up with an email summarising your points.
